## 完整说明 Full description（≤4000 字符）

```
🍬 Sweet Crush — the sweetest match-3 adventure!

Swap, match and pop glossy candies across 35+ handcrafted levels. Line up 3 or more treats to clear the board, chain dazzling cascades, and fill your screen with beams, blasts and lightning!

🎯 THREE SWEET GAME MODES
• Classic — hit the target score in limited moves (20 levels)
• Collect — gather every candy on the list (15 levels)
• Timed — 60 seconds of pure score rush. Beat your own record!

✨ CRAFT MIGHTY SPECIAL CANDIES
• Striped Candy — blasts an entire row or column
• Wrapped Candy — explodes twice in a 3x3 blast
• Color Bomb — clears every candy of one color
Combine two specials for spectacular MEGA effects — double color bombs wipe the whole board!

🧊 CRUNCHY OBSTACLES
Break through ice to free frozen candies and crush stubborn cookies with nearby matches. Fresh board shapes keep every level interesting: crosses, donuts, pyramids, diamonds and twin towers.

🔨 HANDY BOOSTERS
Stuck? Smash a single candy with the Hammer, clear a 3x3 area with the Bomb, or remix the whole board with Shuffle.

⭐ EARN ALL THREE STARS
Score big to earn up to three stars on every level. Progress is saved on your device — replay any level to perfect your rating.

📱 SWEET BY DESIGN
• Silky-smooth animations: bursting particles, light beams, shockwaves and screen shake
• Juicy sound effects and haptic feedback
• Crisp vector art that looks sharp on every screen
• Localized in English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, 日本語 and 한국어

🚀 PLAY ANYWHERE
No internet connection needed — enjoy Sweet Crush on the plane, on the subway or on the couch. Free to play, no ads.

Ready for a sugar rush? Grab your candies and start crushing! 🍭
```

- 文案已避开 Play 审核违禁用语（"#1"、"Best"、"Install Now" 等推广性声明/CTA）。
- "Free to play, no ads"：当前项目无广告 SDK、无内购，属实；若日后接入广告或内购，
  记得同步修改此句。
- 关卡数（20 经典 + 15 收集 + 1 限时 = 36）与截图文案 "35+" 一致；新增关卡后可更新。
